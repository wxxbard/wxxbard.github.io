X ARCH STUDIO DECLARATION

X ARCH STUDIO tries to stand on the opposite side of the building and look at the building, trying to find the uniqueness of space and time under a specific field, in a strategic way to establish interaction with the surrounding environment. "X" is the "world" where (non) buildings appear in the lake / sea water. This "world" is sometimes calm and clear, sometimes microwave ripples, and sometimes surging. Sometimes it is reflected in the moon, sometimes the smoke is empty, and sometimes it is shattered and broken. This seems to be because of the wind. "X" is the intersection and encounter between the two trajectories "/" and "\"; it is a metaphor for material encounters such as wall and floor, wall and wall during the construction process; it is the cross combination of architecture, city and world; it is the cross collision between real space and fictional space(The "/" is used to metaphorize the real space system, which is material, clear, and existing; the "\" is used to metaphorize the fictional space system, which is immaterial, fuzzy, and absent).At the junction of "/" and "\", it represents the true beginning of the story and the emergence of new things, representing goals, hopes and surprises.
X ARCH STUDIO wanders the edge of the real world and the ideological world, looking for the truth of existence.
