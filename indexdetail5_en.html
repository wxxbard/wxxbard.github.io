Continue LanTing Again

Project Name: Continue LanTing Again (Design of Desheng International Center Office, Xicheng District, Beijing)

Project Location: Desheng International Center, Xicheng District, Beijing

Design area: 2800㎡

Design year: 2019

The interior design is based on the WenZhengMing’s LanTing series of paintings in the Ming Dynasty. It aims to seek the space poetry in the "LanTing Collection" and the spiritual civilization of the oriental tradition in Wang Xizhi's novels. The design draws the boundaries of the desk with natural river curves to create a spatial and spatial context. The shape of the desk is cloud-like and movable. The green plants placed in the office area bring garden-like scenery, wild taste and atmosphere to the whole indoor environment. Here, the design breaks the tedious pattern of traditional office space to stimulate the vitality, passion and creativity of the office space of the new era, advocating and seeking some freedom. To create a sense of home, the company is both a home and a garden.
