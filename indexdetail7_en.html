House of Mountains (Stone)

This building base is located in the mountains, and the surrounding natural environment is beautiful. The concept of the architectural design is that the building is a delicate "stone" located in the mountains, and through the appropriate cutting of a complete cuboid, an architectural work suitable for this unique environment is created. The surface of the building is brushed to create a simple, natural and thick surface texture, creating a rough atmosphere and echoing the surrounding natural environment.
