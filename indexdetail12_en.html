Landscape Architecture, Spatial Embedded

    Guangming Port is located in Fuzhou City and belongs to the tributary of Minjiang River. The base of the building is located between the park and the river port, which can be regarded as a transition zone between land and water. The architectural design takes protection as a strategy, and appropriately hides the building with an embedded method to reduce the damage of the new building to the park landscape and water quality landscape, create different spatial roaming paths, connect the original park road to the building roof, form a viewing platform, and create a waterfront space and hydrophilic platform at the boundary between the river and the building.
