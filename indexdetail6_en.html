Vague Space Homestay

        Project Name:Vague Space Homestay

        Project Location: YanQing District, Beijing

        Design area: 400㎡

        Design year: 2019

        The design of the Vague Space Homestay uses simple materials, using plain cement, bamboo poles, metal mesh, steel, wood and other elements to create a quiet and elegant atmosphere of the space. The careful arrangement of the wooden grille, spaced bamboo poles, and metal mesh brings the space Light and misty mood. The style of the entire homestay is a modernist homestay style, which respects the living habits of modern people while being regionalist.
